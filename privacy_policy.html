Last Updated: July 20, 2025

1. What Data We Collect

  • User joins via invites (who invited whom)
  • Voice channel activity, including who moved whom
  • Deleted messages and who deleted them (if available)
  • Temporary voice channels created when users join the "Create" channel

2. Why We Collect This Data

The data is collected to:

  • Help server admins track moderation actions
  • Maintain server transparency and accountability
  • Provide automated temporary voice channels with moderation controls

3. Where the Data is Stored

All logs are posted to dedicated Discord channels within your server. No data is stored outside Discord or in external databases.

4. Who Can Access This Data

Only members with access to the log channels in your Discord server. The bot developers do not access or store your server's data.

5. Your Rights

You may remove the bot at any time. Deleting the bot removes all its activity and permissions from your server.

6. Contact

If you have questions or need support, please contact the developer via Discord.
